Experience

I am Njamu Koni, the founder and owner of CherryC, a retail business I established in 2020. Prior to launching Cherry, I worked as a baker at Tamboja from 2017 to 2020, specializing in 3D cake modeling and figurine design. My passion for creativity and innovation extends across various mediums, including sound engineering, photography, and skateboarding.

Currently, I am a sound engineer at Bamboo Music Production, a role I have held since 2023. In 2024, I worked as an assistant on the impactful art exhibition Mapping Colonial History by Sithabile Mlotshwa at the Visual Arts Council in Lusaka, Zambia.

My hobbies include skateboarding, photography, filming/documenting, and music.

I am driven by an insatiable curiosity to learn about diverse topics and have a strong goal of creating interconnected, self-sustaining projects and systems through my wide array of skills.
